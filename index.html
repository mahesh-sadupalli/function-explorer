Key properties

  • Monotonic if m ≠ 0; constant if m = 0
  • Continuous, unbounded
  • Derivative y' = m (constant)
  • Second derivative = 0 → convex and concave
  • Lipschitz constant = |m|

Intercepts

y-intercept at (0, b). If m ≠ 0, x-intercept at (−b/m, 0).

How to read the slope

Triangle shows rise over run. Positive m tilts up to the right; negative m tilts down.
